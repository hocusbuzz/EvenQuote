and phone number to that specific business. Once shared, that
business may contact you directly outside of EvenQuote — and how
they handle your data is governed by their own policies, not ours.

Treat every quote as a **starting point for a conversation**, not a
binding offer. **Always confirm the price, scope, and terms in
writing with the pro before you pay them anything.**
